What Do County Chairs Do?

County Chairs are the frontlines of the Ohio Republican Party. They are elected or appointed to lead local operations, organize volunteers, support candidates, and run effective Get Out The Vote efforts.

  • Coordinate local Republican events and meetings
  • Support school board and municipal candidates
  • Recruit volunteers for election operations
  • Liaise with the State Central Committee
  • Provide voter education and promote civic engagement
